inlinestatic
Parameters
[in]grainthe grain on which the GlidePlane is defined
[in]Pa point on the plane
[in]Nthe normal to the plane
Returns
the key which uniquely identifies the plane. The type of the key is a tuple with entries (grainID,r,h), where r is the ReciprocalLatticeDirection corresponding to N, and h=P.dot(r) is an integer indicating the "heigth" of the plane from the origin, in integer multiples of the interplanar distance d=1/|r|.
