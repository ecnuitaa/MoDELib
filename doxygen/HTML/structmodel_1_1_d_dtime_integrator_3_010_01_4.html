inlinestatic

Compute and store DislocaitonNode velocities

Computes the time step size \(dt\) for the current simulation step, based on maximum nodal velocity \(v_{max}\).

The time step is calculated according to:

\[ dt= \begin{cases} \frac{dx}{v_{max}} & v_{max} > fc_s\\ \frac{dx}{fc_s} & v_{max} \le fc_s\\ \end{cases} \]

where \(c_s\) is the shear velocity and \(f=0.1\) is a constant.
